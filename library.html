GLP-1 and Related Medications

GLP-1

Semaglutide (Ozempic, Wegovy)

A weekly GLP-1 receptor agonist commonly tracked for metabolic support and weight-management workflows.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Dose day consistency and delays
  • Appetite changes across week phases
  • GI symptoms, hydration, and meal tolerance

GIP + GLP-1

Tirzepatide (Mounjaro, Zepbound)

A weekly dual agonist protocol often monitored with weight trends, hunger response, and tolerance patterns.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Weekly adherence and injection timing
  • Energy changes around dose adjustments
  • Symptom trend stability over multiple weeks

GLP-1

Dulaglutide (Trulicity)

A long-acting weekly GLP-1 entry with emphasis on routine consistency and dose-tolerance observations.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Missed-dose recovery and schedule normalization
  • Body weight trend direction over time
  • Recurring symptom timing windows

GLP-1

Liraglutide (Victoza, Saxenda)

A daily GLP-1 option where adherence and injection-site rotation logs are especially important.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Daily dose completion streaks
  • Injection site rotation quality
  • Day-to-day appetite and symptom swings

Therapeutic Peptides

Peptide

BPC-157

Frequently used in self-tracking contexts focused on recovery narratives and localized response notes.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Protocol cadence and consistency
  • Target area notes with timeline context
  • Subjective recovery and discomfort trends

Peptide

TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)

Often tracked as part of staged protocols where users want clean logs around timing and symptom progression.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Loading and maintenance phase timing
  • Perceived mobility or recovery changes
  • Any side-effect markers during stack use

Peptide Stack

CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin

A common stacked workflow where timing and frequency structure are key to meaningful record quality.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Stack timing alignment and adherence
  • Sleep quality and recovery notes
  • Body composition trends over longer windows

Peptide

Sermorelin

Typically monitored for routine consistency and symptom-level feedback over extended use periods.

What to track in ShotClock

  • Dose consistency across weeks
  • Sleep and energy markers
  • Provider-facing summary notes
